Physical AI presents extraordinary challenges comparable to autonomous vehicle development, rooted in the physical world's inherent variability. Objects deform and conditions change unpredictably, causing systems that succeed in controlled settings to fail on real-world irregularities like damaged boxes or uneven surfaces.
