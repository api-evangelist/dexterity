Dexterity unveiled Foresight, its production-grade world model for robotic manipulation, trained on over 100 million real-world autonomous actions in enterprise logistics. Unlike traditional world models built for observation, Foresight is designed for manipulation, prioritizing interpretability, safety, and speed for industrial deployment.
